The MOBIL-ID project is an engineering systems capstone project for Oklahoma Christian University. The team’s mission statement is to create a mobile platform front-end to Oklahoma Christian University’s student management system. Students and Faculty will use their mobile ID to gain chapel attendance, enter the university cafeteria, and pay using Eagle Bucks.
